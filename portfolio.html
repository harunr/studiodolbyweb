Nature's Farms

Brand Identity, Packaging, Illustration

GWK, the farmer-owned South African agribusiness operating from its head office in the Northern Cape, approached us to create a new, proudly South African farmer brand under the name Nature's Farms. Their newly created facility is one of the most modern and technologically-advanced new food production facilities on the African continent and consists of a wheat mill, pasta plant and biscuit bakery boasting the lates and most advanced manufacturing technology imported from ltaly and Switzerland.

Client did not want to see the usual generic looking package designs and was looking for something unexpected and contemporary to tell the beautiful story of how their farmers harvest the goodness of nature, to bring you the very best. By naming each of the products afrer a farm in the area where they sourced the raw material, the hoped to capture the same magical quality and craftsmanship that goes into making a fine wine in the way they brought the product story to life.
